About Me

I’m now a Associate Professor in Tianjin University, co-learding the Deep Reinforcement Learning Lab, founded by Prof. Jianye Hao (郝建业). Before that, I was a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological Univesrsity (NTU) and worked in the research group of Prof. Liu Yang (刘杨).
